Jack Fehrenbacher

Front-end / Development / Design

Graphic design My interest in making and designing was piqued at the age of 14 when I got my hands on Adobe Photoshop and InDesign CS1 in a high school yearbook class. The end game was always to make things that did things, to put it bluntly, so trying my hand at web development and design, trying to understand how the things that I create will be used rather than just viewed, seemed like a natural progression.

I was born and raised in North Idaho I grew up in Moscow, ID, and while I enjoy and love the things that Idaho has to offer (mostly its emptiness), at 18 it felt important to be somewhere with more people, more water, and more opportunities. Seattle fit the bill.

I have some useful skills! I spent the first two years of my academic career in Seattle University's digital design department, and I've spent the last year of my life in the School of New and Continuing Studies' Web Application and Technologies program learning how to bring my designs to life.
